Exposition of dependent origination Namo tassa Bhagavato Arahato sammā sambuddhassa
I pay homage with devotion at the feet of my blessed, enlightened Sammāsambuddha who arrived at the omniscient
state, overcoming the cycle of births and deaths by realising Dhamma such as dependent origination which has three periods (kālatra) such as the past, twelve factors (dvādasāńga) such
as ignorance, twenty modes (vīsatākāra), three connections (tisandhi), four groups (rāshi), three rounds (tini vațța), two roots (dve mulā) at the foot of the Sri Mahā bodhi while standing on the
earth called morality (sila), with two feet called effort (viriya), having in the hand called faith (saddha), the axe called knowledge of extinction of craving (kammakkhaya ñāna), sharpened on the
stone slab called concentration (samādhi) by the strength of the
thirty perfections (pāramī) completed over a long period of four asankheyya (1 and 140 zeros) and one hundred thousand aeons;
and describe in the Hela (Sinhala) language, the deep Dhamma
of dependent origination taught by that blessed one, to the best of
my ability in order to show the path to Nibbana to the Dhamma
loving meritorious people desirous of overcoming suffering in
sansāra. May the people read and understand this deep Dhamma
lovingly and attempt to realise Nibbāna!!
